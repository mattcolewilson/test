About this Website

Hello, my name is Matt. I’m a big fan of video games and the indie web, so making an independent website about video games seemed like a great idea. Aside from being a lifelong player of games, I don’t have any real credentials to plug. But I’ve got a lot of enthusiasm, and that’s gotta be enough to have a website, right?

This site is called BSSFGHT because it looks cool and the domain hadn’t yet been taken. It’s a good name! It’s maybe a little aggro, what with those big bold letters and all. But I’m leaning into it. (It can also be viewed as vaguely anti-capitalist, if you try hard enough.) Check out the intro post if you want more backstory and thanks for stopping by.

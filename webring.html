BSTRDS Network

The BSTRDS Network is a small webring for people who feel alienated by the mainstream internet and social media landscape. This is a modest attempt to encourage and connect with likeminded netizens and carve out a small corner of cyberspace for us to share.

In order to be accepted, your site must meet the following criteria:

Your content is personal and non-commercial.
This is not a place for storefronts or portfolios. All sites listed here should be personal and non-commercial in nature.

Your site rejects the mainstream internet.
Your site should have some element of hand-made creation and ideally does not rely on corporations.

No bigots or centrists allowed.
This is, unsurprisingly, the least negotiable rule.

Interested in joining?
Send a link to your website to info@bstrds.net.

The Fine Print

This is a webring by the loosest definition. There is no widget available and you aren’t even required to link back to this page. (Although, feel free!) If you are a purist who is bothered by the haphazard use of the term, feel free to refer to this as a “network” or a “club.” Please note, this is not a link directory. All sites listed here have asked to join and have been vetted to meet the above criteria.

The BSTRDS Network was launched in the Spring of 2023.
